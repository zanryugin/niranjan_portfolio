About Me

I am Niranjan Kannanugo, a seasoned graphic designer with two years of experience in the field. I have a strong background in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, print and design, and CSS. Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to collaborate with major companies and work on personal projects. My passion lies in taking experiences to the next level and amazing people through my designs.

Even when the results fall short of my ideal expectations, I find immense pleasure in creating something out of nothing. My expertise includes graphic design, UI/UX, prototyping, and leadership. I hold a B.Tech degree in Computer Science & Engineering and I am fluent in multiple languages. In my portfolio, you will find my work in website front-end design, packaging and branding, as well as content creation. I am committed to delivering excellence in every project I undertake, and my dedication to the craft of design sets me apart.
